The cards

Cards are the main asset of the game.
There are 40 different noble cards, and 50 different action cards.

Nobles cards

Noble cards are worth varying points depending on their prestige. Collecting noble cards is the primary way to build up your score.
Few noble cards cause also additional side effects when drawn.
